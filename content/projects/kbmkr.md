# kbmkr

A desktop-native keyboard-design toolkit that wraps [Ergogen](https://ergogen.xyz)
with CLI + MCP support and talks to KiCad tooling. The companion hardware repo,
`kb-cornish-flippsie`, is the real-world test case.

Built in the open on [GitHub](https://github.com/twish/kbmkr).
